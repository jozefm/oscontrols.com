Who We Are

Learn about us.

Today’s energy market is fast-paced. Your success depends on engineering services to help solve your unique issues.

From complex projects to simple system upgrades to challenging performance issues, POWER’s solutions are tailored to meet your technical needs—while keeping your project on time and on budget.

Your Trusted Advisor

With the largest dedicated power delivery engineering staff of any U.S. consultant and offices nationwide, we offer a strong local and regional presence, backed by a deep pool of resources.

Plus, POWER brings more than 40 years of industry-leading experience in engineering, construction management and environmental coordination to your project.

Our goal is to listen to you, understand your concerns and help you make the best decisions for your system. We work hard to become your trusted advisor, someone who will work seamlessly with your staff to deliver the specialized solutions you need.

Partner with POWER

Our long-term relationships and solid record of success are why most of our clients call on POWER again and again.
